About ProgQuiz

ProgQuiz is an interactive platform designed to help individuals test and improve their programming skills. Our quizzes cover various programming languages and offer different difficulty levels to cater to both beginners and advanced learners.

We believe that practice is key to mastering programming, and our goal is to provide a fun and engaging way to practice coding skills.
